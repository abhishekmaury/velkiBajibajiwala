Hansika Motwani is an Indian actress who primarily appears in Tamil and Telugu films. She began her career as a child actor in Hindi films before making her debut as a lead actress in the Telugu film "Desamuduru" in 2007, Kantri (2008), and Maska (2009). Since then, she has starred in several successful Tamil and Telugu films such as "Engeyum Kadhal" and "Aambala". She has also appeared in various television shows and serials. Hansika has won several awards for her performances and is also a popular brand ambassador

Apart from her acting career, Hansika is also known for her philanthropic work. She has been involved in several initiatives aimed at improving the lives of underprivileged children and women who are suffering from breast cancer.

In her personal life, Hansika is known for her love for animals. She is an advocate for animal welfare and has been associated with several animal welfare organisations. Hansika is also a fitness enthusiast and regularly shares her workout routines on social media

With her talent, passion, and philanthropy, Hansika Motwani is not just an accomplished actress but also an inspiration and role model to many.
